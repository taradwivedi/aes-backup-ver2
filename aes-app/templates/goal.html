Project Goals

What is the Goal?

The primary goal of our Automated Essay Scoring System is to bridge the gap between human evaluation and instant, objective feedback. We aim to provide a tool that not only scores essays accurately but also guides users toward better writing through detailed, actionable feedback.

Need for an AES System

  • Scalability: Manual essay grading is time-consuming and subjective. AES systems can evaluate thousands of essays in seconds, ensuring consistency and fairness.
  • Instant Feedback: Students benefit from immediate insights, allowing them to learn and improve iteratively.
  • Support for Educators: Teachers can focus on higher-level instruction while the system handles routine evaluation.
  • Accessibility: Anyone, anywhere, can access high-quality writing feedback without waiting for a human grader.
  • Data-Driven Insights: AES systems can identify common writing issues across large groups, helping educators tailor their teaching.

Why This Project is Unique

Unlike basic spellcheckers or rule-based grammar tools, our AES system integrates a deep learning LSTM model with advanced feature extraction and real-time grammar feedback. This hybrid approach allows for nuanced scoring and personalized suggestions, making the system both powerful and user-friendly.
